About Me

I'm Bojan Božović, a game designer who discovered his passion for creating interactive experiences at age 14 through game modding. What started as curiosity about how games work evolved into a career dedicated to crafting memorable player experiences. Today, as Team Lead at Replai, I combine my mathematical background with creative design to build games that entertain millions, with multiple titles achieving Top 10 Grossing status on mobile platforms.

Interests, ambitions and a bit more

My first programming experience - a "Snowball Fight" Lua script mod for multiplayer PC game - CounterStrike2D was officially accepted on unrealsoftware.de, where I later became an official USGN modder and game designer. I contributed to another script which saw use in over a dozen active servers reaching over 100 active players.

Throughout high school, I won multiple regional mathematics competitions and later worked as a math tutor during university, teaching children mathematical thinking and problem-solving.
Even today i check up on my students and we keep in touch - mostly when they find some mathematical brain teasers.

During university, I designed and playtested my own original board games, I'd later create a digital versions for Anrdoid. This cross-medium approach strengthened my understanding of core mechanics and iterative design.
I continue to maintain a collection of game prototypes, constantly experimenting with new engagement strategies.
